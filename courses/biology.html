What is the aim of IB Biology?

Students by the end of this course should be knowledgeable about micro and macro aspects of various life forms. This course is more knowledge based and therefore IB expects students taking this course to demonstrate depth and clarity when providing explanations.

IB Biology SYLLABUS

Both IB Biology SL and HL consist of the same core requirements (95 hours). Both classes cover the same six topics in the order listed below. However, HL requires students to study a few additional topics, also listed below.

SL & HL HL (additional 60 hours)
Cell Biology Nucleic Acids
Molecular Biology Metabolism, Cell Respiration & Photosynthesis
Genetics Plant Biology
Ecology Genetics & Evolution
Evolution and Biodiversity Animal Physiology

In addition to the aforementioned topics, you will have to study one additional topic from the options below. For any option, SL will require you to study three of four sub-topics (a total of 15 hours of study), whereas HL will require an additional two or three sub-topics (25 hours of study in total for HL).

SL & HL (hours) HL (additional hours)
Neurobiology & Behavior 15 10
Biotechnology & Bioinformatics 15 10
Ecology & Conservation 15 10
Human Physiology 15 10
