Who Should take Math AA?

Based on the characteristics of Math AA and Math AI, we can deduce that Math AA is for students who are generally interested in not just solving math but also knowing the background. This means students who are curious about pure Mathematical theories, how formulas are derived and proving concepts, would be the right fit for Math AA. This course will help prepare students for Math-intensive fields such as Engineering, Machine Learning etc.

Who should take Math AI?

Those students on the other hand who are not too eager about Pure Mathematics but ‘have’ to take it for their respective future study Program’s requirement, would be best suited to Math AI. This course is not very pure Mathematics-oriented and it will show you fascinating practical implementations of Mathematics in various fields such as Finance, Business, Intelligence etc by using methods such as Modelling.

Owing to the recent advancements in various technological sectors such as Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, etc, the International Baccalaureate Program has introduced IB Mathematics Analysis and Approaches (IB Math AA) and IB Mathematics Applications and Interpretations (IB Math AI) to make IB Mathematics more relevant and practical.

We understand the choosing process of the two Mathematics courses can be a daunting task for you but we have provided below some basic differences and features of the two courses that can help you make your decision.
