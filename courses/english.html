What is the aim of IB Physics?

IB Physics aims to reveal insight upon the most fundamental experimental science, which explains and simplifies matters ranging from the smallest of particles to complex concepts such as the functionality of the universe.

Importance of taking IB Physics

Physics is one of the core subjects for anyone entering the field of Engineering. As challenging as this subject gets, it provides students with answers to their curiosity regarding practical life matters and helps equip them with knowledge necessary to build a solid foundation for their engineering careers ahead.
